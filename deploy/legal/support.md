## Frequently asked questions

**How do I sign in?**
Use the email and password provided by Ardent Networks. A one‑time verification
code (OTP) may be sent to your registered email or mobile number to complete
sign‑in. There is no public self‑registration — accounts are provisioned by
Ardent Networks.

**I forgot my password.**
On the login screen, tap **Forgot Password** and follow the instructions sent to
your registered email. If you do not receive the email, check your spam folder or
contact support.

**I did not receive my OTP / verification code.**
Wait a moment and request the code again. Make sure your registered email and
mobile number are correct. If the problem continues, contact support.

**I can't access my account or my role is wrong.**
Account access and roles (customer, end user, agent, biller, admin) are managed
by Ardent Networks. Contact support to have your account reviewed or updated.

**Questions about billing or my subscription.**
Billing and subscription details shown in the App are for reference. For billing
concerns, payments, or plan changes, contact support or your Ardent Networks
account representative.

**How is my data handled?**
See our [Privacy Policy](https://starlink.ardentnetworks.com.ph/privacy-policy)
and [Terms of Service](https://starlink.ardentnetworks.com.ph/terms).

**How do I request account deletion?**
Email support@ardentnetworks.com.ph with your account details to request deletion
of your account and associated personal data, subject to any legal retention
requirements.
