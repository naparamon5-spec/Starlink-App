# Privacy Policy

**Ardent Dashboard**

_Last updated: July 22, 2026_

Ardent Networks Inc. ("Ardent Networks," "we," "us," or "our") operates the
**Ardent Dashboard** mobile and web application (the "App"), a private
account‑management tool for our internet and Starlink‑connectivity services.
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through the App, how we
use it, and the choices you have. We process personal data in accordance with
the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173), its
Implementing Rules and Regulations, and the issuances of the National Privacy
Commission (NPC).

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of
information as described in this Policy.

---

## 1. Who this Policy applies to

The App is a login‑only tool provided to Ardent Networks customers, end users,
and authorized staff (administrators, agents, and billers). You must have
credentials issued or approved by Ardent Networks to use it. There is no public
self‑registration.

## 2. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to operate your account and provide our
services.

**a. Information you or your provider give us**
- **Account and identity data:** name, email address, mobile number, customer or
  account number, service address, and user role.
- **Authentication data:** password (stored only in hashed form) and one‑time
  verification codes (OTP).
- **Billing and subscription data:** plan details, billing status, invoices,
  payment records, and transaction history. _Note: the App displays billing
  information; we do not collect or store full card numbers within the App._
- **Support content:** tickets, messages, and files or attachments you submit.

**b. Information collected automatically**
- **Device and technical data:** device model, operating system version, app
  version, and a device identifier used to keep you securely signed in and to
  deliver notifications.
- **Usage and diagnostic data:** log events and error reports used to keep the
  App reliable and secure.

We do **not** collect precise location, contacts, photos, or files beyond those
you explicitly choose to upload or download.

## 3. How we use your information

We use personal data to:
- authenticate you and secure your account;
- display and manage your subscription, billing, and service information;
- process and respond to support tickets and requests;
- send service‑related notifications (for example, OTPs, billing reminders, and
  account updates);
- operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the App; and
- comply with legal, regulatory, and audit obligations.

We rely on the following legal bases under the Data Privacy Act: performance of
our contract with you, your consent, our legitimate interests in operating and
securing the service, and compliance with legal obligations.

## 4. How we share information

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only:
- **within Ardent Networks**, with authorized staff who need it to service your
  account;
- **with service providers** (for example, hosting, messaging/OTP delivery, and
  analytics) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations;
- **when required by law**, or to protect the rights, safety, and property of
  Ardent Networks, our customers, or the public.

## 5. Data storage and security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS/TLS) connections and stored on
secured servers. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes. We apply
organizational, physical, and technical safeguards appropriate to the
sensitivity of the data. No method of transmission or storage is completely
secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data retention

We keep personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to
provide the service, then for the period required to meet legal, tax,
accounting, and regulatory obligations. When data is no longer needed, we
securely delete or anonymize it.

## 7. Your rights

Subject to the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to be informed, to access,
to correct (rectify), to object to or restrict processing, to erasure or
blocking, to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with the National
Privacy Commission. You may also withdraw consent where processing is based on
consent.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 11.

## 8. Children's privacy

The App is intended for account holders and authorized users and is not directed
to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

## 9. Account deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated personal data, contact us at
the email in Section 11. We will process the request subject to any legal
retention requirements described in Section 6.

## 10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on
this page with a revised "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notified to
you in‑App or by email. Continued use of the App after changes take effect
constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
